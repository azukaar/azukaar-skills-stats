# Minecraft Mod: Complete Skill Tree Design

## **MAIN SKILL TREES**

### **ARTISAN** *(Mining → Crafting & Resource Mastery)*
**Description/Lore:** Masters of creation and material manipulation. Artisans understand the hidden properties of materials and can coax extraordinary results from ordinary tools. They see potential where others see raw stone, and their touch can transform the mundane into the magical.

**General Idea:** Focus on crafting efficiency, resource enhancement, tool mastery, and material transformation. Skills that make gathering and creating more rewarding.

**Example Skills:**
- **Early - Quick Repair:** Instantly restore 25% durability to held tool (2min cooldown)
- **Early - Efficient Hands:** Tools lose durability 25% slower
- **Mid - Green Thumb:** Crops grow 50% faster near you, bone meal works twice as effectively
- **Mid - Material Insight:** See ore veins through stone within 5 blocks, know exact ore types
- **Mid - Master Craftsman:** 15% chance to not consume materials when crafting
- **Late - Transmutation:** Convert materials (iron→gold, stone→obsidian) with limited daily uses
- **Late - Forge Master:** Create unique enhanced versions of tools/armor with special properties

---

### **MEDIC** *(Vitality → Healing & Protection)*
**Description/Lore:** Guardians of life and wellness. Medics have learned to channel their own vitality to heal wounds and cure ailments. They stand as beacons of hope in the darkest caves and most dangerous adventures, ensuring no one falls to poison or despair.

**General Idea:** Active healing abilities, status effect management, group support, and ultimate immunity to harmful effects.

**Example Skills:**
- **Early - First Aid:** Heal yourself for 3 hearts (2min cooldown)
- **Early - Hardy Constitution:** Poison/wither effects last 50% less time
- **Mid - Regeneration:** Slowly heal when below half health
- **Mid - Group Heal:** Heal nearby players and pets simultaneously (5min cooldown)
- **Mid - Antidote:** Remove all negative effects from self
- **Late - Life Link:** Share damage taken with nearby willing players
- **Late - Immunity:** Complete immunity to poison, wither, and hunger effects

---

### **BARBARIAN** *(Strength → Raw Power & Intimidation)*
**Description/Lore:** Primal warriors who embrace the fury of battle. Barbarians tap into ancient rage to overwhelm their enemies through sheer force and intimidation. They are living weapons, feared by monsters and revered by allies.

**General Idea:** Burst damage abilities, intimidation effects, weapon mastery, and berserker mechanics that reward aggressive play.

**Example Skills:**
- **Early - Rage:** +100% attack damage for 8 seconds (3min cooldown)
- **Early - Powerful Strikes:** +2 base attack damage with melee weapons
- **Mid - Weapon Focus:** Choose weapon type, gain +3 additional damage with it
- **Mid - Intimidating Presence:** Nearby mobs flee when you kill their allies
- **Mid - Berserker:** When below 50% health, gain +50% attack speed and damage
- **Mid - Cleave:** Melee attacks hit multiple enemies in front of you
- **Late - War Cry:** AoE fear effect, weakens enemy attacks for 30 seconds
- **Late - Unstoppable Force:** 10 seconds of immunity to knockback + massive damage (10min cooldown)

---

### **GUARDIAN** *(Armor → Defense & Protection)*
**Description/Lore:** Stalwart defenders who stand between danger and those they protect. Guardians have mastered the art of defense, turning their bodies into living fortresses. They draw enemy attacks and shrug off damage that would fell lesser warriors.

**General Idea:** Damage reduction, shield mastery, taunt mechanics, and protective abilities for solo and group play.

**Example Skills:**
- **Early - Defensive Stance:** Toggle: -50% movement, -60% damage taken
- **Early - Stalwart:** -15% damage from all sources
- **Mid - Shield Mastery:** Shields block 100% damage instead of partial
- **Mid - Taunt:** Force nearby mobs to attack you instead of allies
- **Mid - Armor Expertise:** Armor provides 25% more protection
- **Late - Protective Aura:** Nearby allies take 20% less damage
- **Late - Fortress:** Become immobile but nearly invulnerable for 10 seconds (15min cooldown)

---

### **ROGUE** *(Dexterity → Precision & Status Effects)*
**Description/Lore:** Masters of precision and subtlety. Rogues strike from the shadows with calculated precision, using stealth and cunning to overcome enemies. They excel at finding weaknesses and exploiting them with deadly efficiency.

**General Idea:** Stealth mechanics, critical hit enhancement, status effects (poison/bleed), and precision-based combat.

**Example Skills:**
- **Early - Stealth:** 5 seconds of invisibility (45sec cooldown)
- **Early - Keen Eye:** +25% critical hit chance
- **Mid - Steady Aim:** Bow accuracy improved, less arrow drop
- **Mid - Poison Blade:** Melee attacks apply poison for 10 seconds
- **Mid - Bleeding Edge:** Critical hits cause bleeding damage over time
- **Late - Multishot Mastery:** Bows fire 2-3 arrows without multishot enchant
- **Late - Shadow Strike:** Teleport behind enemy and deal massive critical damage

---

### **SURVIVALIST** *(Endurance → Sustenance & Environment)*
**Description/Lore:** Hardy individuals who thrive in the harshest conditions. Survivalists have adapted to live off the land and endure what would kill others. They find sustenance in unlikely places and weather any storm.

**General Idea:** Food efficiency, environmental adaptation, alternative sustenance methods, and resistance to harsh conditions.

**Example Skills:**
- **Early - Cannibalism:** Consume 2 hearts to restore full hunger/saturation (1min cooldown)
- **Early - Iron Stomach:** Eat raw food safely, +25% nutrition from all food
- **Mid - Efficient Metabolism:** Food restores 50% more hunger/saturation
- **Mid - Night Vision:** Permanent low-level night vision
- **Mid - Forager:** Harvest berries/mushrooms from environment for food
- **Mid - Weather Proof:** No movement penalty in rain/snow, resist cold/heat damage
- **Late - Hibernation:** Sleep anywhere, gain health while sleeping
- **Late - One With Nature:** Slowly regenerate health/hunger in natural environments

---

### **NOMAD** *(Agility → Movement & Traversal)*
**Description/Lore:** Wanderers who have mastered the art of movement. Nomads are never truly lost and never truly trapped. They flow like water around obstacles and soar like birds above the landscape, always seeking the next horizon.

**General Idea:** Enhanced mobility, traversal abilities, mount bonuses, and movement-based skills that make exploration faster and safer.

**Example Skills:**
- **Early - Dash:** Burst forward 10 blocks instantly (30sec cooldown)
- **Early - Fleet Footed:** +25% movement speed
- **Mid - Soft Landing:** -75% fall damage, no damage under 10 blocks
- **Mid - Aquatic:** Swim speed matches land speed, hold breath 3x longer
- **Mid - Climber:** Climb any surface like a spider
- **Mid - Beast Friend:** Tamed animals move faster and take less damage
- **Late - Parkour:** Wall-running, double-jump, enhanced mobility
- **Late - Wind Walker:** Brief flight ability (10 seconds, 5min cooldown)

---

### **SCHOLAR** *(Luck → Knowledge & Enhancement)*
**Description/Lore:** Seekers of hidden knowledge and cosmic understanding. Scholars perceive patterns others miss and bend probability through understanding. They unlock the secrets of enchantment and reveal the hidden properties of the world.

**General Idea:** Information gathering, enchanting improvements, probability manipulation, and discovery mechanics.

**Example Skills:**
- **Early - Analyze:** Reveal all properties of targeted block/item/mob (30sec cooldown)
- **Early - Student:** +25% XP from all sources
- **Mid - Appraiser:** See exact durability, enchantments, and hidden properties
- **Mid - Enchanter's Wisdom:** Enchanting costs 25% less XP with better results
- **Mid - Treasure Sense:** Detect valuable blocks/chests within 20 blocks
- **Mid - Recipe Master:** Unlock hidden/advanced crafting recipes
- **Late - Probability Control:** Reroll any random event once per day (loot, enchants, etc.)
- **Late - Omniscient:** Reveal all information about items, mobs, and environments

---

## **BONUS ACHIEVEMENT TREES**

### **ENDER MASTERY** *(Unlocked: Kill Ender Dragon)*
**Description/Lore:** Those who have conquered the End and stared into the void itself. Ender Masters manipulate space and dimension, wielding powers that transcend the mortal realm. They walk between worlds and command the forces that bind reality.

**General Idea:** Three branches based on archetypes, each focusing on dimensional manipulation through their specialty - combat teleportation for warriors, dimensional storage for miners, and planar travel for explorers.

**Example Skills:**
- **Warrior - Void Strike:** Teleport to enemy and deal massive damage
- **Warrior - Ender Wrath:** Void Strike hits all enemies in 5-block radius
- **Miner - Void Mining:** Mine in 5x5x5 area, blocks go to dimensional storage
- **Miner - Dimensional Quarry:** Void Mining works across dimensions, auto-sorts materials
- **Explorer - Far Sight:** Teleport to any location you can see
- **Explorer - Planar Walker:** Open temporary portals between any two visited locations

---

### **NETHER MASTERY** *(Unlocked: Kill Wither)*
**Description/Lore:** Conquerors of the underworld who have claimed dominion over fire and soul. Nether Masters harness the primal forces of heat and destruction, bending hellfire to their will and walking unharmed through landscapes of molten death.

**General Idea:** Three archetype branches focusing on hellish mastery - soul-powered combat for warriors, lava/heat manipulation for miners, and infernal traversal for explorers.

**Example Skills:**
- **Warrior - Soul Weapon:** Weapons gain fire damage and steal enemy souls for buffs
- **Warrior - Infernal Arsenal:** Soul weapons create fire explosions and grant fire immunity while held
- **Miner - Lava Forge:** Craft with lava as fuel, create obsidian tools/armor
- **Miner - Magma Mastery:** Swim in lava, mine at normal speed in lava, collect magma blocks
- **Explorer - Heat Walker:** Immune to fire/lava, walk on lava surface
- **Explorer - Nether Lord:** Fly in Nether, see through lava, nether mobs won't attack unless provoked

## **PARAGON CLASSES** *(Advanced Hybrid Trees)*

### **PALADIN** *(Medic + Guardian)*
**Requirements:** Level 20+ in both Medic and Guardian trees
**Description/Lore:** Holy warriors who merge healing arts with protective duty. Paladins are living sanctuaries, radiating divine protection while wielding righteous power against evil. They stand as beacons of hope on the battlefield, equally capable of mending wounds and turning undead.

**General Idea:** Protective healing, auras that benefit groups, anti-undead abilities, becoming a mobile fortress of healing and protection.

**Example Skills:**
- **Holy Aura:** Nearby allies slowly regenerate health and take less damage
- **Turn Undead:** Undead mobs flee in terror or take massive damage
- **Divine Strike:** Attacks heal nearby allies while damaging enemies
- **Sanctuary:** Create a temporary safe zone where no hostile mobs can enter
- **Martyrdom:** Absorb all damage taken by nearby allies for a short duration

---

### **TAMER** *(Nomad + Survivalist)*
**Requirements:** Level 20+ in both Nomad and Survivalist trees
**Description/Lore:** Masters of the wild who speak the ancient language of beasts. Tamers have transcended the boundary between civilized and feral, commanding respect from all creatures and moving through nature as one with it.

**General Idea:** Animal companions, beast communication, pack mechanics, temporary animal transformations, and enhanced interaction with all creatures.

**Example Skills:**
- **Beast Whisper:** Communicate with and temporarily befriend wild animals
- **Pack Leader:** Summon wolf companions that fight alongside you
- **Shapeshifter:** Transform into animals (wolf for speed, bear for strength, bird for flight)
- **Call of the Wild:** All nearby animals become temporary allies
- **Primal Bond:** Take hits for your pets

---

### **ASSASSIN** *(Barbarian + Rogue)*
**Requirements:** Level 20+ in both Barbarian and Rogue trees
**Description/Lore:** Perfect killers who combine brute force with surgical precision. Assassins have mastered the art of the perfect kill, striking from shadows with overwhelming power or overwhelming their enemies with precise, calculated fury.

**General Idea:** Instant kill mechanics, combo attacks, stealth bursts, execution abilities, and overwhelming single-target damage.

**Example Skills:**
- **Mark for Death:** Target takes +200% damage from all sources for 30 seconds
- **Stealth Strike:** Next attack from stealth deals 500% damage and applies all status effects
- **Fury Combo:** Each consecutive hit increases damage exponentially
- **Silent Rage:** Enter berserk mode while remaining invisible
- **Execute:** Instantly kill enemies below 25% health

---

### **ARTIFICER** *(Artisan + Scholar)*
**Requirements:** Level 20+ in both Artisan and Scholar trees
**Description/Lore:** Legendary crafters who blend ancient knowledge with masterful creation. Artificers don't just make items—they breathe life and magic into their creations, crafting wonders that defy natural law.

**General Idea:** Magical item creation, temporary enchantments, automation/golems, item analysis and improvement, and reality-bending craftsmanship.

**Example Skills:**
- **Enchant Weapon:** Temporarily add powerful enchantments to any weapon/tool
- **Craft Golem:** Create an iron golem companion that follows and protects you
- **Analyze & Improve:** Permanently enhance existing items with new properties
- **Transmutation Circle:** Convert any material into any other material (expensive)
- **Arcane Workshop:** Create a portable crafting station with unlimited resources for 1 hour
