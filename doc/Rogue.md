## **Overview**
The Rogue tree focuses on stealth, precision combat, health-spending abilities, and gaining satisfaction from violence. It features five main progression paths with strategic Warrior Aspect gates to ensure meaningful progression.

---

## **Core Entry Skills** *(No Aspect Requirements)*

### **Stealth** (2sp, max 3)
- **Effect:** 10 seconds of invisibility per level (1min cooldown)
- **Max Duration:** 30 seconds at level 3
- **Purpose:** Foundation for stealth and positioning

### **Keen Eye** (1sp, max 3)
- **Effect:** +10% critical hit chance per level
- **Max Benefit:** 30% increased crit chance
- **Purpose:** Foundation for precision combat

### **Combat High** (2sp, max 3)
- **Effect:** Activate to heal for 50% of damage dealt for 5 seconds per level (2min cooldown)
- **Max Duration:** 15 seconds at level 3
- **Purpose:** Foundation for sadistic healing mechanics

### **Fleet Footed** (1sp, max 3)
- **Effect:** +10% movement speed per level for 30 seconds after hitting an enemy
- **Max Benefit:** 30% combat speed increase
- **Purpose:** Enhanced combat mobility

### **Adrenaline Rush** (2sp)
- **Effect:** Spend 2 hearts to gain +20% damage and speed for 1 minute (3min cooldown)
- **Purpose:** Foundation for health-spending abilities

## **Critical Mastery Branch**

### **Precision Training** (1sp, max 3)
- **Prerequisite:** Keen Eye
- **Effect:** +15% critical damage per level
- **Max Benefit:** 45% increased crit damage

### **Steady Aim** (2sp)
- **Prerequisites:** Keen Eye level 2
- **Effect:** Bow accuracy improved, 50% less arrow drop
- **Ranged:** Enhanced projectile precision

### **Bleeding Edge** (2sp) ðŸ”’
- **Prerequisites:** Precision Training + **Warrior Aspect 30**
- **Effect:** Critical hits cause bleeding (1 heart per 3 seconds for 15 seconds)
- **DoT:** Damage over time application

### **Lethal Precision** (3sp)
- **Prerequisites:** Steady Aim + Bleeding Edge
- **Effect:** Critical hits with any weapon have 25% chance to cause bleeding
- **Expansion:** Universal bleeding application

### **Multishot Mastery** (4sp) ðŸ”’
- **Prerequisites:** Lethal Precision + **Warrior Aspect 70**
- **Effect:** Bows fire 2-3 arrows without multishot enchant, each can crit independently
- **Ultimate:** Enhanced ranged damage

## **Sadistic Healing Branch**

### **Bloodlust** (2sp)
- **Prerequisite:** Combat High
- **Effect:** Critical hits auto-activate Combat High for 10 seconds
- **Synergy:** Automatic activation on precision strikes

### **Pain Tolerance** (1sp, max 3)
- **Prerequisite:** Combat High
- **Effect:** Take 10% less damage per level when Combat High is active
- **Max Benefit:** 30% damage reduction during Combat High
- **Defensive:** Protection during healing mode

### **Enhanced High** (2sp)
- **Prerequisites:** Bloodlust + Pain Tolerance
- **Effect:** Combat High heals for 75% of damage dealt instead of 50%
- **Enhancement:** Improved healing efficiency

### **Sadistic Glee** (2sp)
- **Prerequisites:** Enhanced High + Pain Tolerance level 2
- **Effect:** While Combat High is active, gain temporary damage bonus for each enemy killed (stacks up to 5 times, +20% per stack, lasts until Combat High ends)
- **Stacking:** Kill streak bonuses during Combat High

### **Killing Euphoria** (3sp) ðŸ”’
- **Prerequisites:** Sadistic Glee + **Warrior Aspect 50**
- **Effect:** When Combat High is active, killing enemies extends Combat High duration by 5 seconds and grants burst healing (2 hearts instantly)
- **Ultimate:** Death-triggered healing and duration extension

### **Psychokiller** (4sp) ðŸ”’
- **Prerequisites:** Killing Euphoria + **Warrior Aspect 60**
- **Effect:** Below 25% health while Combat High is active: +100% damage, +50% speed, +50% crit chance
- **Ultimate:** Near-death power surge during Combat High

## **Status Warfare Branch**

### **Poison Blade** (2sp) ðŸ”’
- **Prerequisites:** Adrenaline Rush + **Warrior Aspect 20**
- **Effect:** Spend 1 heart to coat weapon with poison for 60 seconds (poison lasts 10 seconds per hit)
- **Application:** Health-cost poison enhancement

### **Toxic Mastery** (2sp)
- **Prerequisite:** Poison Blade
- **Effect:** Poison duration doubled and reduces enemy damage by 25%
- **Enhancement:** Improved poison effects

### **Extended Toxin** (2sp)
- **Prerequisite:** Toxic Mastery
- **Effect:** Poison Blade lasts 3 minutes instead of 60 seconds, poison application lasts 20 seconds per hit
- **Duration:** Longer poison effects

### **Crippling Strikes** (3sp)
- **Prerequisites:** Extended Toxin + Bleeding Edge
- **Effect:** While Poison Blade is active, attacks also apply weakness and slowness for 15 seconds
- **Debuff:** Movement and damage reduction when poisoning

### **Death Mark** (5sp) ðŸ”’
- **Prerequisites:** Crippling Strikes + **Warrior Aspect 80**
- **Effect:** Mark target enemy - they take +200% damage from all sources for 30 seconds (costs 4 hearts, 10min cooldown)
- **Ultimate:** Team damage amplification

## **Sacrificial Power Branch**

### **Blood Price** (2sp)
- **Prerequisites:** Adrenaline Rush + Pain Tolerance
- **Effect:** Spend health to reduce all ability cooldowns (1 heart = 10 seconds reduction)
- **Resource:** Health-to-time conversion

### **Desperate Fury** (3sp)
- **Prerequisites:** Blood Price + Enhanced High
- **Effect:** When below 25% health, all abilities cost no health for 15 seconds (triggers once per combat)
- **Emergency:** Free ability usage

### **Life Burn** (3sp) ðŸ”’
- **Prerequisites:** Desperate Fury + **Warrior Aspect 40**
- **Effect:** Spend up to 6 hearts to gain massive temporary benefits (+25% per heart: damage, speed, crit chance) for 30 seconds
- **Scaling:** Variable health investment

### **Soul Sacrifice** (4sp) ðŸ”’
- **Prerequisites:** Life Burn + **Warrior Aspect 70**
- **Effect:** Reduce to 1 heart to become invulnerable for 10 seconds with +200% damage (once per day)
- **Ultimate:** Near-death invincibility

### **Phoenix Rising** (5sp) ðŸ”’
- **Prerequisites:** Soul Sacrifice + Death Mark + **Warrior Aspect 80**
- **Effect:** If you would die, instead return to full health and gain all benefits for 30 seconds (20min cooldown)
- **Ultimate:** Death prevention with power surge
