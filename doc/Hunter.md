# **HUNTER** *(Paragon: Farmer + Barbarian)*

## **Core Philosophy:**
**Beast Master** - Ultimate animal control and pack coordination  
**Resource Bridge** - Connects Farmer's production with Barbarian's consumption  
**Apex Predator** - Hunting, tracking, and pack dynamics

---

## **Requirements**
- **Farmer Tree:** Level 30+
- **Barbarian Tree:** Level 30+
- **Combined Investment:** Minimum 60 skill points across both prerequisite trees

---

## **Foundation Skills**

### **Pack Leader** (3sp) - **CORE ABILITY**
- **Prerequisites:** Farmer "Animal Whisperer" level 3
- **Effect:** Animal Whisperer now creates temporary pack members instead of just attraction - attracted animals attack what you attack for 5 minutes
- **Pack Bonus:** +5% damage and speed for each pack animal (caps at +50% with 10 animals)
- **Purpose:** Foundation for all Hunter abilities - turns attraction into combat alliance

### **Pack Commands** (1sp)
- **Prerequisites:** Pack Leader
- **Effect:** Command your pack to stay in place or follow you (toggle command)
- **Control:** Basic pack management

### **Beast Summoning** (3sp)
- **Prerequisites:** Pack Leader + Farmer "Nature's Call" + Barbarian "Iron Stomach"
- **Effect:** Summon any type of animal for combat, last 5 minutes (10min cooldown)
- **Combat:** Flexible animal creation with short downtime

## **Beast Mastery Branch**

### **Primal Bond** (3sp)
- **Prerequisites:** Beast Summoning + Barbarian "Rage"
- **Effect:** Share your Barbarian abilities with animal companions at 50% effectiveness
- **Synergy:** Animals gain rage bonuses

### **Alpha's Pack** (4sp)
- **Prerequisites:** Primal Bond + Farmer "Mass Breeding"
- **Effect:** Mass Breeding also affects summoned animals and wild animals in range
- **Population:** Instant animal army creation

### **Beast Coordination** (4sp)
- **Prerequisites:** Alpha's Pack + Barbarian "War Cry"
