# SENTINEL *(Paragon: Engineer + Nomad)*

## **Overview**
The Sentinel paragon class represents the ultimate fusion of defensive engineering and tactical mobility. These masters of mobile fortification can rapidly establish defensive positions, create teleportation networks, and deploy advanced battlefield technology. The Sentinel offers two distinct ultimate paths: Fortress Commander or Mech Pilot.

---

## **Requirements**
- **Engineer Tree:** Level 30+ 
- **Nomad Tree:** Level 30+
- **Combined Investment:** Minimum 60 skill points across both prerequisite trees

---

## **Foundation Skills** *(Early Paragon Access)*

### **Mobile Deployment** (3sp) - **CORE ABILITY**
- **Prerequisites:** Engineer "Field Fortress" + Nomad "Portal Stones"
- **Effect:** Automatically place a 4th portal stone inside each fortress you build
- **Synergy:** Each fortress becomes part of your teleportation network

### **Shared Network** (3sp)
- **Prerequisites:** Mobile Deployment
- **Effect:** Other players can use your portal stones for teleportation (they still suffer their own vulnerability windows)
- **Teamwork:** Extends your teleportation network to allies

### **Tactical Positioning** (2sp)
- **Prerequisites:** Mobile Deployment + Engineer "Auto-Turret"
- **Effect:** When using Emergency Escape, automatically deploy a basic turret at departure location
- **Integration:** Emergency evacuation leaves defensive coverage behind

## **Fortress Commander Path**

### **Rapid Deployment** (3sp)
- **Prerequisites:** Shared Network + Engineer "Fortified Walls"
- **Effect:** Field Fortress deployment becomes instant (no delay) and removes movement penalty
- **Efficiency:** Instant fortress deployment without vulnerability

### **Advanced Amenities** (3sp)
- **Prerequisites:** Rapid Deployment
- **Effect:** Fortresses also include furnace and chest storage with 3 cooked steaks and 3 coal
- **Utility:** Extended base functionality with basic supplies

### **Iron Workshop** (3sp)
- **Prerequisites:** Advanced Amenities
- **Effect:** Fortresses include anvil and chest contains 3 iron ingots and wooden sticks for basic iron tools
- **Utility:** Advanced crafting capabilities with starter materials

## **Mech Pilot Path**

### **Mech Assembly** (5sp) - **ALTERNATE PILLAR SKILL** ðŸ”’
- **Prerequisites:** Engineer "Turret Network" + Nomad "Emergency Landing" + **Mining Aspect 70** + **Explorer Aspect 70**
- **Effect:** 10-second transformation into mech suit with following stats:
  - **Defense:** +200% damage reduction
  - **Speed:** +50% movement speed  
  - **Health:** Independent 50-heart health bar
  - **Duration:** Permanent until destroyed or dismissed
  - **Cooldown:** 20 minutes after destruction/dismissal

### **Mech Weapons** (3sp)
- **Prerequisites:** Mech Assembly
- **Effect:** Mech gains shoulder-mounted turret that fires automatically at nearby enemies
- **Integration:** Combines Engineer turret tech with mech platform

### **Mech Jetpack** (3sp)
- **Prerequisites:** Mech Assembly
- **Effect:** Hold space for short flight bursts (3-second flight, 10-second recharge)
- **Enhancement:** Aerial mech mobility

### **Deflector Array** (2sp, max 3)
- **Prerequisites:** Mech Assembly
- **Effect:** 5% damage deflection per level while in mech form
- **Max Benefit:** 15% damage deflection at level 3
- **Scaling:** Progressive defensive enhancement

### **Omni-Dash** (3sp)
- **Prerequisites:** Mech Jetpack + Nomad "Dash" level 3
- **Effect:** Mech-only ability - dash in any direction including vertically (uses Nomad dash cooldown and distance)
- **Enhancement:** Full 3D mobility while in mech form

### **Plasma Burst** (4sp) ðŸ”’
- **Prerequisites:** Mech Weapons + Deflector Array + **Mining Aspect 80**
- **Effect:** Mech-only ability - knockdown and damage all hostiles in 10-block radius (2min cooldown)
- **Ultimate:** Area damage and crowd control

### **Auto-Repair** (4sp) ðŸ”’
- **Prerequisites:** Omni-Dash + Deflector Array + **Explorer Aspect 80**
- **Effect:** Mech-only ability - stand still to slowly regenerate mech health (1 heart per 3 seconds)
- **Ultimate:** Self-maintenance capability

## **Synergy Integration Branch**

### **Speed Compensation** (2sp)
- **Prerequisites:** Mobile Deployment + Nomad "Fleet Footed"
- **Effect:** Engineer speed penalties reduced by 8% per level of Fleet Footed you have
- **Balance:** Nomad training reduces Engineer mobility weakness

### **Protected Mobility** (3sp)
- **Prerequisites:** Speed Compensation + Engineer "Powered Shield"
- **Effect:** No teleport vulnerability while carrying an active shield
- **Synergy:** Engineer protection completely negates Nomad vulnerability

### **Fortress Recall** (4sp) ðŸ”’
- **Prerequisites:** Protected Mobility + **Mining Aspect 60** + **Explorer Aspect 60**
- **Effect:** Emergency Escape can target any fortress portal stone instead of just nearest portal
- **Ultimate:** Controlled emergency evacuation to fortified positions

- **Engineer fortress costs:** Standard redstone for turrets and powered abilities

- **Path Selection:** Fortress network vs individual power

## **Total Investment**
- **Maximum possible skill points:** ~55 SP if everything is maxed
- **Fortress Commander specialization:** 20-30 SP for complete base management
- **Mech Pilot specialization:** 25-35 SP for full mech capabilities  
- **Synergy Integration:** 10-15 SP for Engineer/Nomad compensation
- **Hybrid builds:** 35-45 SP across both paths (not recommended)
- **Entry level:** 10-15 SP for basic Engineer/Nomad synergy and team portal sharing
- **Complete Sentinel:** 40+ SP for mastery of chosen path

## **Core Philosophy**
The Sentinel transforms the Engineer/Nomad dependency into mutual enhancement while adding new strategic dimensions. Fortress Commanders create persistent infrastructure that benefits entire teams, while Mech Pilots become mobile heavy support platforms. Both paths maintain the tactical depth of the base classes while providing unique endgame fantasies that reward massive skill point investment.
