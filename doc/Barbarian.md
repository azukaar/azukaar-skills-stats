## **Core Entry Skills** *(No Aspect Requirements)*

### **Rage** (2sp, max 3)
- **Effect:** +50% damage per level for 10 seconds (3min cooldown)
- **Max Benefit:** +150% damage at level 3
- **Purpose:** Foundation burst damage ability

### **Powerful Strikes** (1sp, max 3)
- **Effect:** +1 base attack damage per level with melee weapons
- **Max Benefit:** +3 base damage
- **Purpose:** Permanent damage increase

### **Iron Stomach** (1sp)
- **Effect:** Eat raw meat safely as if it were cooked, no hunger/poison effects
- **Purpose:** Foundation for food consumption abilities

## **Food Consumption Branch**

### **Gluttony** (4sp) - **PILLAR SKILL**
- **Prerequisites:** Iron Stomach + Rage
- **Effect:** Consume all food in inventory - gain +2% damage per saturation point and +1 heart per food point (2min duration, caps at +400% damage)
- **Scaling:** Massive temporary power through food consumption

### **Ravenous Appetite** (1sp, max 3)
- **Prerequisites:** Gluttony
- **Effect:** Rage duration increased by 33% per level, extend current rage by 1 second per food point consumed (caps at +50 seconds per level)
- **Max Benefits:** +100% rage duration, +150 seconds extension cap
- **Enhancement:** Passive rage improvement and food-based extension

### **Survivor's Feast** (3sp) ðŸ”’
- **Prerequisites:** Ravenous Appetite + **Warrior Aspect 20**
- **Effect:** Emergency consumption when below 25% health - heal up to 10 hearts by consuming food (2min cooldown)
- **Emergency:** Massive desperate recovery

### **Carnivore's Strength** (4sp) ðŸ”’
- **Prerequisites:** Survivor's Feast + **Warrior Aspect 50**
- **Effect:** Improves Gluttony when consuming meat - gain +2% damage per food point instead of +1% (damage caps at +400%, speed penalty -1% per food point, caps at -50%)
- **Enhancement:** *Enhanced by Hunter skills for reliable meat supply*
- **Ultimate:** Meat consumption doubles Gluttony effectiveness

### **Savage Feast** (3sp) ðŸ”’
- **Prerequisites:** Carnivore's Strength
- **Effect:** Consume meat - gain +1% attack speed and +0.5% crit chance per saturation point (45sec duration, caps at +200% speed/+100% crit)
- **Enhancement:** *Enhanced by Hunter skills for sustainable meat source*
- **Speed:** Alternative to raw damage

### **Alpha's Dominance** (4sp) ðŸ”’
- **Prerequisites:** Savage Feast + **Warrior Aspect 70**
- **Effect:** Consume meat - create intimidation aura with 1-block radius / 1 sec duration per food point where animals become passive/flee (caps at 1min duration / 60-block radius)
- **Enhancement:** *Enhanced by Hunter pack mechanics*
- **Ultimate:** Massive area control
