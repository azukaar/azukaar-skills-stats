# Artisan Skill

## **Overview**
The Artisan tree is unlocked through Mining stat progression and focuses on crafting mastery, resource enhancement, material manipulation, and tool specialization. It features five main progression paths with strategic Miner Aspect gates to ensure meaningful progression.

---

## **Core Entry Skills** *(No Aspect Requirements)*

### **Ironwright's Touch** (1sp, max 3)
- **Effect:** Restore 25% durability to held tool (10min cooldown)
- **Scaling:** Each level improves the base repair ability
- **Purpose:** Foundation for the entire durability/forging branch

### **Stone's Secret** (1sp, max 5)
- **Effect:** Reveal ores within 10 blocks per level for 10 seconds (1min cooldown)
- **Max Range:** 50 blocks at level 5
- **Purpose:** Foundation for mining enhancement and automation

### **Woodland Craft** (2sp)
- **Effect:** Create a basic wooden tool from nothing (60sec cooldown per tool type, costs XP)
- **Tools Available:** Swords, pickaxes, axes, shovels, hoes
- **Purpose:** Emergency tool access and enchanting fodder

### **Portable Bench** (2sp)
- **Prerequisite:** Woodland Craft
- **Effect:** Place a crafting table from thin air (costs XP, 1min cooldown)
- **Utility:** Instant crafting access without carrying materials

### **Traveling Furnace** (3sp)
- **Prerequisite:** Portable Bench
- **Effect:** Place a furnace from thin air (costs XP, 1min cooldown)
- **Advanced:** Instant smelting setup anywhere

### **Master's Anvil** (5sp)
- **Prerequisite:** Traveling Furnace
- **Effect:** Place an anvil from thin air (costs XP, 1min cooldown)
- **Ultimate:** Complete instant workshop deployment

## **Durability & Forging Branch**

### **Enduring Craft** (1sp, max 5)
- **Prerequisite:** Ironwright's Touch
- **Effect:** Tools lose 5% less durability per level
- **Max Benefit:** 25% durability loss reduction

### **Forge-sworn Hands** (3sp)
- **Prerequisites:** Ironwright's Touch level 3
- **Effect:** Ironwright's Touch now restores 50% durability instead of 25%
- **Synergy:** Doubles the effectiveness of your basic repair ability

### **Eternal Anvil** (5sp) ðŸ”’
- **Prerequisites:** Grand Forge + **Miner Aspect 80**
- **Effect:** Choose ONCE between Tools/Weapons/Armor - selected type never loses durability (COST XP)
- **Strategic Choice:** Permanent solution for one equipment category

## **Mining & Automation Branch**

### **Iron Fist** (2sp)
- **Prerequisite:** Stone's Secret
- **Effect:** Hand mining at iron pickaxe level for 30 seconds (3min cooldown)
- **Synergy:** Combines with Mining Frenzy for rapid resource gathering

### **Wooden Expertise** (2sp)
- **Prerequisite:** Iron Fist
- **Effect:** +5% efficiency with wooden tools
- **Foundation:** Entry point for tool specialization mastery

### **Stone Expertise** (2sp)
- **Prerequisite:** Wooden Expertise
- **Effect:** +5% efficiency with stone tools
- **Stacking:** Combines with Wooden Expertise for multi-tier bonuses

### **Golden Touch** (2sp)
- **Prerequisite:** Stone Expertise
- **Effect:** +5% efficiency with golden tools
- **Note:** Golden tools maintain their speed advantage with added efficiency

### **Diamond Mastery** (2sp)
- **Prerequisite:** Golden Touch
- **Effect:** +5% efficiency with diamond tools
- **Elite:** High-tier tool optimization

### **Universal Craft** (2sp)
- **Prerequisite:** Diamond Mastery
- **Effect:** +5% efficiency with ALL tool types
- **Capstone:** Ultimate tool efficiency across all materials

### **Expertise Amplifier** (1sp, max 5)
- **Prerequisite:** Universal Craft
- **Effect:** +5% bonus efficiency per level to ALL unlocked tool specializations
- **Max Benefit:** +25% amplification to your entire specialization tree
- **Example:** With all specializations + max amplifier: Wood tools get +30% efficiency (5% base + 25% amplifier)

### **Fortune's Focus** (2sp)
- **Prerequisites:** Stone's Secret level 3
- **Effect:** +50% effectiveness of Fortune enchantment
- **Example:** Fortune III becomes ~4.5x drops instead of 3x

### **Vein Walker** (2sp)
- **Prerequisites:** Stone's Secret level 2
- **Effect:** Stone's Secret cooldown reduced by half
- **Benefit:** More frequent ore detection

### **Mining Frenzy** (3sp)
- **Prerequisite:** Vein Walker
- **Effect:** +300% mining speed for 15 seconds (5min cooldown)
- **Synergy:** Perfect combo with Iron Fist for burst mining

### **Earth's Eye** (5sp) ðŸ”’
- **Prerequisites:** Stone's Secret level 5 + Vein Walker + **Miner Aspect 70**
- **Effect:** Stone's Secret becomes permanent toggle (no cooldown)
- **Impact:** Constant ore vision when active

### **Infernal Forge** (2sp) ðŸ”’
- **Prerequisites:** Forge-sworn Hands + **Miner Aspect 30**
- **Effect:** Instantly smelt off-hand stack (1min cooldown)
- **Usage:** Convert raw ores to ingots on-demand

### **Forge Mastery** (2sp)
- **Prerequisite:** Infernal Forge
- **Effect:** Infernal Forge cooldown reduced by half (30 seconds)

### **Auto-Smelt** (2sp)
- **Prerequisite:** Infernal Forge
- **Effect:** Toggle - Mined ores automatically smelt into ingots
- **QoL:** Eliminates need for manual smelting

### **Stone Discard** (1sp)
- **Prerequisite:** Auto-Smelt
- **Effect:** Toggle - Automatically discard mined stone/cobblestone
- **QoL:** Keeps inventory clean during mining

### **Armor Smith** (3sp)
- **Prerequisite:** Forge Mastery
- **Effect:** Infernal Forge now works on armor pieces
- **Expansion:** Extends smelting to armor repair/enhancement

### **Grand Forge** (4sp) ðŸ”’
- **Prerequisites:** Armor Smith + **Miner Aspect 60**
- **Effect:** Infernal Forge repairs entire inventory
- **Ultimate:** Mass repair solution

### **Alchemical Mastery** (5sp) ðŸ”’
- **Prerequisites:** Earth's Eye + **Miner Aspect 80**
- **Effect:** Convert materials via predetermined transmutation recipes (cost: XP)
- **Examples:** All Stones, to any other stones

## **Primitive Crafting Branch**

### **Efficient Crafting** (1sp, max 5)
- **Prerequisite:** Woodland Craft
- **Effect:** Reduce tool creation cooldown by 12 seconds per level
- **Max Benefit:** 60 seconds reduction
- **Universal:** Affects ALL material tiers

### **Stonework Mastery** (2sp) ðŸ”’
- **Prerequisites:** Woodland Craft + **Miner Aspect 20**
- **Effect:** Create stone tools from nothing (75sec cooldown per tool type, costs XP, affected by Efficient Crafting)

### **Ironwork Mastery** (3sp) ðŸ”’
- **Prerequisites:** Stonework Mastery + **Miner Aspect 40**
- **Effect:** Create iron tools from nothing (90sec cooldown per tool type, costs XP, 75% durability)
- **Note:** Tools start with reduced durability but can be enhanced

### **Reinforced Iron** (2sp)
- **Prerequisite:** Ironwork Mastery
- **Effect:** Iron tools created have full durability instead of 75%
