# Artificer

## **Overview**
The Artificer tree is a Paragon class unlocked by reaching level 20+ in both Artisan and Scholar trees. It represents the ultimate fusion of material mastery and arcane knowledge, focusing on advanced crafting, cross-tree synergies, and reality-bending abilities.

---

## **Requirements**
- **Artisan Tree:** Level 30+ 
- **Scholar Tree:** Level 30+
- **Combined Investment:** Minimum 60 skill points across both prerequisite trees

---

## **Foundation Skills** *(Early Access)*

### **Learned Craft** (2sp)
- **Effect:** Artisan's "Efficient Crafting" now affects ALL crafting recipes, not just primitive tools
- **Benefit:** Universal material efficiency across all crafting types
- **Purpose:** Foundation for advanced material work

---

## **Cross-Tree Synergy Branch**

### **Basic Synergy** (3sp)
- **Prerequisites:** Artisan basic skills + Scholar basic skills
- **Effect 1:** Scholar's XP bonus increases by +5% per 10 Artisan skill points you have
- **Effect 2:** Artisan's "Stone's Secret" range increases by +5 blocks per Scholar detection skill point
- **Purpose:** Foundation synergy between both trees

### **Advanced Synergy** (3sp)
- **Prerequisite:** Basic Synergy
- **Effect 1:** Artisan's "Auto-Smelt" processes +1 additional item per 10 Scholar skill points you have
- **Effect 2:** Scholar's "Structure Sense" range increases by +100 blocks per 10 Artisan skill points you have
- **Purpose:** Mid-level skill enhancement

### **Master Synergy** (4sp)
- **Prerequisite:** Advanced Synergy
- **Effect 1:** Artisan's "Grand Forge" cooldown reduces by 10 seconds per 10 Scholar skill points you have
- **Effect 2:** Scholar's "Ally Teleport" cooldown reduces by 2 minutes per 10 Artisan skill points you have
- **Purpose:** High-level skill acceleration

### **Harmonic Knowledge** (5sp)
- **Prerequisites** Master Synergy + **Explorer Aspect 90** + **Miner Aspect 90**
- **Effect:** Reduce all skill XP costs by 30%, increase all skill XP gains by 30%

### **Harmonic Crafter** (5sp)
- **Prerequisites** Master Synergy + **Explorer Aspect 90** + **Miner Aspect 90**
- **Effect:** Item destroyed by Scholar skills, will give extra XP after destruction (depending on material rarity)

---

## **Advanced Material Branch**

### **Goldwork Mastery** (3sp)
- **Prerequisites:** Learned Craft + Artisan "Ironwork Mastery"
- **Effect:** Create golden tools from nothing (2min cooldown per tool type, costs XP)
- **Enhancement:** Gold tools come with base enchantments

### **Enchanted Edge** (2sp)
- **Prerequisite:** Goldwork Mastery
- **Effect:** Gold tools get Sharpness/Efficiency I

### **Mystic Binding** (2sp)
- **Prerequisite:** Goldwork Mastery
- **Effect:** Gold tools get Unbreaking I

### **Fortune's Touch** (3sp)
- **Prerequisite:** Goldwork Mastery
- **Effect:** Gold tools get Fortune I

### **Looting Mastery** (2sp)
- **Prerequisite:** Goldwork Mastery
- **Effect:** Gold weapons get Looting I

### **Diamondwork Mastery** (4sp)
- **Prerequisites:** ALL four gold enchantment skills (Enchanted Edge + Mystic Binding + Fortune's Touch + Looting Mastery)
- **Effect:** Create diamond tools from nothing (3min cooldown per tool type, costs XP)
- **Enhancement:** Diamond tools come with enhanced durability & multiple enchantments

### **Diamond Enhancement** (2sp)
- **Prerequisite:** Diamondwork Mastery
- **Effect:** All diamond-created tools get +1 enchantment level and Artisan's "Efficient Crafting" reduces their cooldown
- **Example:** Base Fortune I becomes Fortune II, cooldowns reduced by Efficient Crafting bonus

---

## **Transmutation & Alchemy Branch**

### **Philosopher's Transmutation** (3sp)
- **Prerequisites:** Scholar "Philosopher's Stone" + Artisan "Alchemical Mastery"
- **Effect:** Transmutation does not consume the base material anymore
- **Cost:** XP cost scales with material rarity and quantity

### **Efficient Transmutation** (2sp)
- **Prerequisite:** Philosopher's Transmutation
- **Effect:** Transmutation XP costs reduced by 50%
- **Economy:** Makes transmutation more sustainable

---

## **Advanced Abilities Branch**

### **Burst Mining** (3sp)
- **Prerequisites:** Artisan "Mining Frenzy" + Scholar detection skills
- **Effect:** When Mining Frenzy is active, mine in 3x3 area instead of single blocks
- **Synergy:** Combines speed and area effects

### **Expanded Burst** (2sp)
- **Prerequisites:** Burst Mining + Scholar detection skills level 3+
- **Effect:** 3x3 becomes 5x5 during Mining Frenzy
- **Scaling:** Larger area mining

### **Echo Crafting** (3sp)
- **Prerequisites:** Advanced material skills + Scholar enchanting skills
- **Effect:** Last crafted item can be instantly created again for free (once per day)
- **Memory:** Knowledge-based duplication

### **Master's Workshop** (5sp)
- **Prerequisites:** Echo Crafting + Master Synergy
- **Effect:** ??

## **Enchantment Mastery Branch**

### **Enchant Extraction** (4sp)
- **Prerequisites:** Artisan "Grand Forge" + Scholar "Safe Extraction"
- **Effect:** 90% chance to transfer one enchantment to a book, 10% chance to destroy the item
- **Innovation:** Enchantment recovery through forging

### **Perfect Extraction** (4sp)
- **Prerequisite:** Enchant Extraction
- **Effect:** Enchant Extraction now has 100% success rate and can extract all enchantments
- **Mastery:** Guaranteed enchantment recovery
