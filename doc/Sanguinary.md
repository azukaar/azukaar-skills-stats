# SANGUINARIAN *(Paragon: Medic + Rogue)*

## **Overview**
The Sanguinarian paragon class represents the ultimate fusion of medical knowledge and combat savagery. These masters of life force circulation can heal allies through violence, share health strategically across teams, and turn the battlefield into a sophisticated network of life energy management.

---

## **Requirements**
- **Medic Tree:** Level 30+ 
- **Rogue Tree:** Level 30+
- **Combined Investment:** Minimum 60 skill points across both prerequisite trees

---

## **Foundation Skills** *(Early Paragon Access)*

### **Life Network** (3sp) - **CORE ABILITY**
- **Effect:** Toggle to create a life-sharing network with up to 5 nearby players (20-block radius)
- **Healing Distribution:** When anyone in network receives healing, it's shared equally among all members
- **Damage Distribution:** When anyone in network takes damage, it's shared equally among all members
- **Basic Sharing:** Network uses simple equal distribution by default
- **Toggle:** Can be turned on/off by network creator
- **Purpose:** Foundation for all advanced Sanguinarian abilities

### **Combat Symbiosis** (2sp)
- **Prerequisites:** Life Network + Rogue "Combat High" + Medic healing skills
- **Effect:** When you activate Combat High, all network members gain 25% of the healing you receive from damage
- **Purpose:** Share your sadistic healing with the team

## **Network Management Examples**

### **Basic Life Network:**
- **Default:** All healing and damage shared equally among 5 members
- **Triage Mode:** Healing goes primarily to lowest health members
- **Allocation Mode:** Damage goes primarily to highest health members
- **Balance Mode:** Master Surgeon equalizes all health instantly

### **Combat High Cascade:**
- **Trigger:** Sanguinarian activates Combat High
- **Network Effects:** 
  - All members gain 25% damage
  - All members with Combat High auto-activate at 75% effectiveness
  - Members share 25% of Sanguinarian's healing from violence
- **Kill Bonus:** Any network kill triggers regeneration for everyone

### **Extraction Network:**
- **Bio-Harvester:** Sanguinarian drains enemy health, 33% goes to network
- **Ocular Injection:** Look at enemy while crouching, drain 1 heart/second to network
- **Enhanced Predator:** Drain from primary target + nearby enemies

## **Core Philosophy**
The Sanguinarian transforms individual combat and healing into a sophisticated team resource management system. Health becomes a shared commodity, combat abilities become team buffs, and the entire group operates as a single organism with distributed life force control. The key is intelligent distribution - healing goes where it's needed most, damage goes where it can be best absorbed, and powerful abilities can be shared strategically across the network.
