## **Overview**
The Engineer tree focuses on defensive structures, protective technology, fortress construction, and resource-driven abilities. It features five main progression paths with strategic Mining Aspect gates and meaningful speed trade-offs that create dependency on mobility classes.


## **Core Entry Skills** *(No Aspect Requirements)*

### **Reinforced Armor** (1sp, max 3)
- **Effect:** Worn armor provides +5% damage reduction per level
- **Max Benefit:** +15% damage reduction at level 3
- **Trade-off:** -5% movement speed per level while wearing armor
- **Purpose:** Foundation defensive enhancement with mobility cost

### **Redstone Reserves** (1sp, max 5)
- **Effect:** Generate 5 redstone per level in off-hand slot when activated (5min cooldown)
- **Max Benefit:** 25 redstone per activation at level 5
- **Purpose:** On-demand resource generation for powered abilities

## **Redstone Engineering Branch**

### **Redstone Mastery** (2sp)
- **Prerequisites:** Redstone Reserves level 2
- **Effect:** Placing redstone dust does not consume it from inventory
- **Enhancement:** Free redstone circuit construction

### **Piston Control** (2sp)
- **Prerequisite:** Redstone Mastery
- **Effect:** Right-click any piston to toggle between normal and sticky piston
- **Utility:** Dynamic piston type switching without rebuilding

### **Circuit Testing** (2sp)
- **Prerequisite:** Redstone Mastery
- **Effect:** Right-click redstone dust on ground to temporarily power it for 10 seconds (30sec cooldown)
- **Utility:** Test redstone circuits without permanent power sources

### **Automated Assembly** (4sp) ðŸ”’
- **Prerequisites:** Piston Control + Circuit Testing + **Mining Aspect 50**
- **Effect:** Spawn geometric shapes of stone blocks - square, circle, or triangle with chosen radius up to 30 blocks (5min cooldown, costs 5 redstone)
- **Ultimate:** Large-scale construction capability

## **Powered Armor Branch**

### **Power Core** (2sp)
- **Prerequisites:** Redstone Reserves level 2 + Reinforced Armor
- **Effect:** Nullify up to 50% of incoming damage by consuming 1 redstone per heart prevented
- **Activation:** Triggers automatically when taking damage if redstone available
- **Side Effect:** Applies Slowness II for 10 seconds when activated
- **Foundation:** Resource-based damage mitigation with mobility penalty

### **Enhanced Plating** (2sp)
- **Prerequisite:** Power Core
- **Effect:** Power Core can now nullify up to 75% of incoming damage
- **Trade-off:** Slowness effect duration increased to 15 seconds
- **Specialization:** Enhanced damage nullification with longer slowness

### **Reactive Armor** (3sp) ðŸ”’
- **Prerequisites:** Enhanced Plating + **Mining Aspect 40**
- **Effect:** 50% chance to reflect all damage nullified by Power Core back to attacker
- **Ultimate:** Retaliatory defense system

## **Shield Technology Branch**

### **Force Barrier** (2sp)
- **Prerequisites:** Reinforced Armor level 2
- **Effect:** While holding shield, create force field around you that blocks projectiles
- **Trade-off:** -25% movement speed while shield is raised
- **Enhancement:** Personal projectile protection

### **Barrier Extension** (2sp)
- **Prerequisite:** Force Barrier
- **Effect:** Force field also protects allied players within 16 blocks
- **Scaling:** Group protection capability

### **Powered Shield** (3sp) ðŸ”’
- **Prerequisites:** Barrier Extension + Power Core + **Mining Aspect 30**
- **Effect:** Nullify up to 100% of incoming damage while shield is raised by consuming 1 redstone per heart prevented
- **Ultimate:** Complete damage negation with resource cost

### **Energy Deflection** (4sp) ðŸ”’
- **Prerequisites:** Powered Shield + **Mining Aspect 60**
- **Effect:** Reflect all damage nullified by Powered Shield back to attackers
- **Ultimate:** Offensive defense capability

## **Turret Deployment Branch**

### **Auto-Turret** (3sp) - **PILLAR SKILL**
- **Prerequisites:** Redstone Reserves level 3
- **Effect:** Deploy dispenser turret that shoots arrows at enemies within 8 blocks (2min duration, 5min cooldown)
- **Consumption:** Costs 10 arrows and 3 redstone per deployment
- **Foundation:** Basic automated defense

### **Targeting System** (2sp)
- **Prerequisite:** Auto-Turret
- **Effect:** Turret range increased to 15 blocks, prioritizes closest enemies
- **Enhancement:** Improved turret effectiveness

### **Ammunition Feed** (2sp)
- **Prerequisite:** Auto-Turret
- **Effect:** Turret duration increased to 5 minutes, costs 5 arrows instead of 10
- **Efficiency:** Longer deployment, cheaper operation

### **Enhanced Ordnance** (2sp)
- **Prerequisites:** Targeting System + Ammunition Feed
- **Effect:** Turret damage increased by 100% and fire rate doubled
- **Upgrade:** Superior turret performance

### **Heavy Ordinance** (3sp)
- **Prerequisites:** Enhanced Ordnance
- **Effect:** Turret fires explosive arrows (causes small explosions)
- **Trade-off:** Deployment cost increased to 5 redstone
- **Upgrade:** Enhanced damage with increased cost

### **Turret Network** (5sp) ðŸ”’
- **Prerequisites:** Heavy Ordinance + **Mining Aspect 70**
- **Effect:** Deploy up to 3 turrets simultaneously, they share targeting data
- **Ultimate:** Multi-turret coordination

## **Resource Economy**

### **Redstone Consumption Rates:**
- **Power Core:** 1 redstone = 1 heart prevented (automatic activation)
- **Powered Shield:** 1 redstone = 1 heart prevented (manual shield raising)
- **Auto-Turret:** 3 redstone base cost, 5 redstone with Heavy Ordinance
- **Redstone Generation:** 25 redstone per 5-minute cooldown (max level)

### **Strategic Resource Decisions:**
- **Defensive vs Offensive:** Spend redstone on damage mitigation or turret deployment
- **Automatic vs Manual:** Power Core triggers automatically, Powered Shield requires active use
- **Efficiency Trade-offs:** Powered Shield costs same but provides complete protection
- **Construction vs Combat:** Use redstone for large-scale building or immediate defense

## **Dependency Relationships**

### **Engineer Weaknesses:**
- **Speed Penalties:** Multiple sources of movement reduction create mobility crisis
- **Vulnerability Windows:** Power Core slowness and fortress deployment create exposure
- **Resource Dependence:** Must manage redstone carefully for sustained operations
- **Construction Limitations:** Large-scale building requires significant redstone investment

### **Nomad Synergy:**
- **Speed Compensation:** Nomad movement skills reduce Engineer speed penalties
- **Protected Mobility:** Engineer shields eliminate Nomad teleport vulnerabilities
- **Emergency Support:** Nomad dash and teleports provide rapid repositioning during Engineer vulnerability windows
- **Construction Support:** Nomad mobility enables rapid material gathering for Engineer building projects
