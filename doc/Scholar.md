# Scholar

## **Overview**
The Scholar tree focuses on knowledge-based abilities including enchantment manipulation, detection and teleportation, treasure hunting, potion crafting, and social interactions. It features five main progression paths with strategic Explorer Aspect gates to ensure meaningful progression.

---

## **Core Entry Skills** *(No Aspect Requirements)*

### **Student** (1sp, max 5)
- **Effect:** Keep +20% XP on death per level
- **Max Benefit:** 50% increased XP at max level
- **Purpose:** Foundation for enchanting branch and overall progression acceleration

### **Life Sense** (1sp, max 3) 
- **Effect:** Detect entities within 5 blocks per level
- **Max Range:** 15 blocks at level 3
- **Purpose:** Entry point for detection and teleportation specialization

### **Treasure Sense** (1sp, max 3)
- **Effect:** Detect chests within 5 blocks per level
- **Max Range:** 15 blocks at level 3
- **Purpose:** Foundation for treasure hunting and exploration

### **Potion Extension** (1sp, max 3)
- **Effect:** +20% potion duration per level
- **Max Benefit:** 60% longer potion effects
- **Purpose:** Entry into alchemy and brewing optimization

### **Merchant's Eye** (1sp, max 2)
- **Effect:** See villager profession & tier within 20 blocks per level
- **Max Range:** 40 blocks at level 2
- **Purpose:** Foundation for social interaction and trading

## **Enchanting Branch**

### **Arcane Understanding** (2sp)
- **Prerequisite:** Student
- **Effect:** Display enchanting probabilities before enchanting
- **Purpose:** Information advantage for enchantment planning

### **Enchanting Efficiency** (1sp, max 3)
- **Prerequisites:** Student level 2
- **Effect:** -5% enchanting XP cost per level
- **Max Benefit:** 15% XP cost reduction

### **Enchant Extraction** (3sp)
- **Prerequisites:** Arcane Understanding + Enchanting Efficiency level 2
- **Effect:** Remove enchantments from items (20% chance to destroy item)
- **Risk/Reward:** Powerful but dangerous enchantment manipulation

### **Safe Extraction** (2sp)
- **Prerequisite:** Enchant Extraction
- **Effect:** Remove destruction risk from Enchant Extraction
- **Safety:** Makes enchantment removal risk-free

### **Enchant Mastery** (2sp)
- **Prerequisite:** Safe Extraction
- **Effect:** 25% chance to refund XP when enchanting
- **Efficiency:** Reduces long-term enchanting costs

### **Arcane Infusion** (4sp) ðŸ”’
- **Prerequisites:** Enchant Mastery + **Explorer Aspect 60**
- **Effect:** Add random enchantment to item (10min cooldown, max 3 per item)
- **Ultimate:** Create powerful multi-enchanted items
