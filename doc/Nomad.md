## **Overview**
The Nomad tree focuses on enhanced mobility, teleportation abilities, portable utility, and exploration enhancement. It features five main progression paths with strategic Explorer Aspect gates and meaningful vulnerability windows that create dependency on defensive classes.

---

## **Core Entry Skills** *(No Aspect Requirements)*

### **Dash** (2sp, max 3)
- **Level 1:** 2 blocks forward, 10sec cooldown, 0.5sec invulnerability
- **Level 2:** 3 blocks forward, 5sec cooldown, 0.5sec invulnerability  
- **Level 3:** 4 blocks forward, 2sec cooldown, 0.5sec invulnerability
- **Purpose:** Foundation mobility with brief damage immunity

### **Fleet Footed** (1sp, max 3)
- **Effect:** +8% movement speed per level
- **Max Benefit:** +24% movement speed at level 3
- **Purpose:** Passive mobility enhancement

### **Soft Landing** (1sp, max 3)
- **Effect:** Reduce fall damage by 25% per level
- **Max Benefit:** 75% fall damage reduction at level 3
- **Purpose:** Vertical mobility safety

## **Teleportation Branch**

### **Recall Home** (3sp) ðŸ”’
- **Prerequisites:** Dash level 2 + **Explorer Aspect 30**
- **Effect:** Teleport to your bed/spawn point (5min cooldown)
- **Vulnerability:** Take +75% damage for 60 seconds after teleport
- **Purpose:** Strategic repositioning with risk

### **Portal Stones** (3sp)
- **Prerequisites:** Recall Home
- **Effect:** Place waypoint markers, teleport between them (3min cooldown between uses)
- **Vulnerability:** Take +30% damage for 60 seconds after teleport
- **Limit:** Maximum 3 portal stones active
- **Utility:** Network of teleportation points

### **Death's Echo** (4sp) ðŸ”’
- **Prerequisites:** Recall Home + **Explorer Aspect 50**
- **Effect:** Teleport to your last death location (10min cooldown)
- **Vulnerability:** Take +75% damage for 60 seconds after teleport
- **Risk/Reward:** High-risk recovery teleport

### **Emergency Escape** (4sp) ðŸ”’
- **Prerequisites:** Portal Stones + **Explorer Aspect 60**
- **Effect:** Auto-teleport to nearest portal stone when below 25% health, automatically place escape route portal at departure location (15min cooldown)
- **Vulnerability:** Take +100% damage for 2 minutes after emergency teleport
- **Emergency:** Automatic survival mechanism with return path

### **Escape Route** (2sp)
- **Prerequisites:** Emergency Escape
- **Effect:** Manually teleport back to last Emergency Escape departure location (works once per escape, 3min cooldown)
- **Utility:** Return path for emergency evacuation

## **Status Resistance Branch**

### **Slowness Resistance** (2sp)
- **Prerequisites:** Fleet Footed + Backpack
- **Effect:** Reduce slowness effect intensity by 50% (Slowness II becomes Slowness I, etc.)
- **Enhancement:** Status effect mitigation

### **Enhanced Resistance** (3sp) ðŸ”’
- **Prerequisites:** Slowness Resistance + **Explorer Aspect 30**
- **Effect:** Reduce slowness effect intensity by additional 25% (75% total reduction)
- **Trade-off:** Improved status resistance

### **Momentum** (3sp)
- **Prerequisites:** Enhanced Resistance + Dash level 3
- **Effect:** Each consecutive dash increases next dash distance by 1 block (stacks 3 times, resets if you stop moving)
- **Scaling:** Accelerating movement capability

### **Emergency Landing** (5sp) ðŸ”’
- **Prerequisites:** Momentum + **Explorer Aspect 80**
- **Effect:** Automatically negates fall damage when triggered (2min cooldown)
- **Ultimate:** Fall damage immunity on cooldown
