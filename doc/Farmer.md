## **Core Entry Skills** *(No Aspect Requirements)*

### **Nature's Call** (1sp, max 5)
- **Effect:** Crops grow 5% faster per level near you
- **Max Benefit:** 25% faster crop growth
- **Purpose:** Foundation for all farming abilities

### **Animal Whisperer** (1sp, max 3)
- **Effect:** Passive animals attracted to you from 10 blocks per level away for 2 minutes. Cooldown 5 minutes.
- **Max Range:** 30 blocks at level 3
- **Purpose:** Foundation for animal husbandry
