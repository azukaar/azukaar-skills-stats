## **Entry Skills** *(No Aspect Requirements)*

### **Nature's Call** (1sp, max 5)
- **Effect:** Crops grow 5% faster per level near you (3 blocks)
- **Max Benefit:** 25% faster crop growth / 15 blocks
- **Purpose:** Foundation for Crop Mastery and Forestry branches

### **Animal Whisperer** (1sp, max 5)
- **Effect:** Passive animals attracted to you from 10 blocks per level away for 2 minutes (5min cooldown)
- **Max Range:** 30 blocks at level 5
- **Purpose:** Foundation for Animal Husbandry branch

## **Crop Mastery Branch**

### **Harvest's Blessing** (1sp, max 5)
- **Prerequisite:** Nature's Call (lvl 2)
- **Aspect Requirement:** Nature 10
- **Effect:** Crops yield 20% more per level when you harvest them
- **Max Benefit:** 20% increased crop yields
- **Type:** Passive

### **Green Touch** (3sp)
- **Prerequisite:** Harvest's Blessing
- **Aspect Requirement:** Nature 20
- **Effect:** Instantly mature one crop (1min cooldown)
- **Type:** Active
- **Purpose:** Active farming acceleration

### **Nature's Domain** (2sp, max 3)
- **Prerequisite:** Green Touch
- **Aspect Requirement:** Nature 30
- **Effect:** Green Touch affects 3x3 area instead of single crop (up to 9x9)
- **Type:** Passive
- **Impact:** Area farming capability

### **Harvest Sweep** (3sp) 
- **Prerequisite:** Nature's Domain
- **Aspect Requirement:** Nature 40
- **Effect:** Instantly harvest all mature crops in 5x5 area (3min cooldown)
- **Type:** Active
- **Purpose:** Core harvesting ability, enhanced by other branches

### **Harvest Area** (2sp, max 5)

### **Fast Planting** (4sp)

## **Harvest Sweep Progression**

The Farmer's core ability evolves as you invest across branches:

1. **Base Harvest Sweep:** Harvest crops in 5x5 area
2. **+ Harvest Area:** Expand to 9x9 area
3. **+ Forest Communion:** Also harvests trees
4. **+ Sapling Nurturing:** Green Touch works on saplings
5. **+ Nature Renewal:** Auto-replants saplings and seeds, reduces animal kills
6. **+ Seed Master:** Seed retention for Fast Planting
7. **+ Herd Communion:** Also loots passive animals (non-lethal chance)
8. **+ Forager's Appetite:** Grants seed-eating buff
